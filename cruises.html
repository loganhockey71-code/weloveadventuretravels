Most Popular

Ocean & Caribbean Cruises

The Caribbean is one of the most popular cruise regions in the world — and for good reason. Seven-night itineraries from ports in Florida and Texas hit multiple islands, give you time in the water, and feel like a completely different world.

Victoria works with all the major cruise lines — Carnival, Royal Caribbean, Norwegian, Celebrity, MSC, and more. She knows which ships are worth the upgrade and which excursions are actually worth booking before you board.

Whether it's your first cruise or your fifteenth, she'll match you with the right ship, the right cabin, and an itinerary that fits your group.

River & Luxury Cruises

River cruises through Europe are an entirely different experience — smaller ships, port-intensive itineraries, and a pace that actually lets you absorb the places you visit. The Danube, the Rhine, and the Douro are favorites Victoria has planned for many clients.

For those who want the finest at sea, luxury lines like Viking, Oceania, Azamara, and Regent Seven Seas offer an all-inclusive experience with exceptional service. Victoria knows these products inside and out and can help you decide if the higher price tag makes sense for your trip.
