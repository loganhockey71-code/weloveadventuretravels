Where Victoria Sends Her Clients

Know Before You Go

What "All-Inclusive" Actually Means

Not all all-inclusives are the same. Victoria helps you understand exactly what's covered before you commit.

Usually Included

  • All meals at main buffet & select à la carte restaurants
  • Domestic bar drinks throughout the day
  • Non-motorized water sports (kayaks, paddleboards)
  • Entertainment & nightly shows
  • Pools, beach access, fitness center
  • Kids club (family resorts)

Sometimes Extra

  • Premium spirits & wine list
  • Specialty restaurants (steak, sushi, etc.)
  • Motorized water sports & scuba
  • Spa treatments
  • Golf
  • Airport transfers

What Victoria Watches For

  • Resort fees hidden in the total price
  • Buffet-only resorts disguised as premium
  • Beach quality vs. pool quality
  • Reviews that don't match the photos
  • Seasonal differences in crowds & weather
  • Travel insurance requirements
